Spinach and Mushroom Omelette

Description

This easy Spinach Mushroom Omelette is a delicious keto vegetarian breakfast recipe easy to whip in 10 minutes any day of the week.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Baby Spinach
  • 1 cup Button Mushrooms fresh, finely sliced (equivalent 4-5 medium mushrooms)
  • 3 teaspoons Butter
  • 1 Small Garlic Cloves peeled and crushed
  • 2 large Eggs beaten
  • 2 teaspoons Olive Oil
  • ¼ teaspoon Salt
  • ¼ cup Shredded Mozzarella or Grated Parmesan
  • 1 pinch Chilli Flakes or ground pepper

Instruction Steps

  1. Crack and beat the eggs in a small bowl. Season with salt, pepper and set aside in the fridge while cooking the vegetables.
  2. Heat a non-stick skillet over medium heat, warm butter. When hot, add mushrooms slices, crushed garlic a pinch of salt and pepper.
  3. Stir fry for about 1-2 minutes, until the mushrooms are roasted and fragrant. Stir in spinach and keep cooking until wilted. Take off the skillet and set it aside on a plate. Cover with a piece of foil to keep warm.
  4. Add olive oil, warm, and spread it evenly into the pan, tilting the pan in a rotating motion.
  5. Reduce to medium-low heat, add beaten eggs and tilt the pan again to spread the egg mixture all over the pan.
  6. When the sides of the omelette are set and start to dry out, lift them with a spatula to let the uncooked eggs from the middle run under and cook.
  7. Once the middle of the omelette starts to set, sprinkle the shredded mozzarella on one half of the omelette and top with cooked spinach and mushrooms.
  8. Fold the omelette in half, reduce to low heat and keep cooking until cheese is melted.
  9. Gently slide on a plate and adjust seasoning, adding more salt, pepper, fresh parsley, or a pinch of chili flakes to taste.
